Welcome to Pet Pet Courier Services!

We are a trusted courier company specializing in the transportation of parcels to and from Lusaka. Whether you are sending a package to family, friends, or business partners, we make shipping simple, fast, and reliable.

To get started, visit our Dashboard and create a shipment order by providing your parcel details, sender information, and destination. Once your order has been submitted, our team will process your shipment and keep you updated throughout the journey.

Every parcel is assigned a unique tracking number, allowing you to monitor its progress at any time. Simply use the Track Parcel feature on our website, enter your tracking number, and instantly view the latest status of your shipment.

At Pet Pet Courier Services, we are committed to delivering your parcels safely and on time. Start your shipment today and experience hassle-free courier services.
